Our Story

DTZ was born in a grimy Boston bar in 2017, when three misfits—Dave, Tony, and Zeke—accidentally started jamming together after the bar’s jukebox died. What began as a spontaneous mess turned into the city’s most chaotic, yet beloved, cover band. Their shows are unpredictable—full of misplaced solos, forgotten lyrics, and the occasional inflatable octopus on stage (thanks, Zeke). Known for their black humor, they often introduce themselves with, “We’re DTZ. Lower your expectations, but you’ll still have a good time.” They don’t write originals, but they’ve perfected the art of playing other people’s hits—just with more mistakes and a lot more laughs.
